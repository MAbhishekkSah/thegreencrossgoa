Rescue and Rehabilitation
Rescue and rehabilitation of wild animals provide medical care, shelter, and support to return them to their natural habitats.
Awareness programmes
Flora and fauna awareness programs educate students on biodiversity, fostering appreciation and proactive conservation efforts in schools and colleges.
Bird Walk and Nature Trail
Bird walks and nature trails offer immersive experiences in observing wildlife, exploring ecosystems, and appreciating natural beauty. Ideal for all ages.
Assisting Students on Thesis
Assisting students with their thesis involves guiding research, refining ideas, providing feedback, and ensuring academic standards for successful completion.
Training sessions
Training on handling snakes involves safety protocols, species identification, and behavior understanding, alongside educational sessions on various reptiles' ecology and habitats.
Raids
We conduct raids on illegal activities like poaching, hunting, displaying snakes, keeping animals in captivity, and confiscating reptiles from snake charmers.
Projects
We undertake projects on awareness programs in collaboration with the Department of Science and Technology, Director of Sports and Youths, and the Forest Department.
Petroling
Conducting patrols with the Goa Forest Department to prevent night hunting, poaching, illegal animal skin trade, and unauthorized tree cutting.
